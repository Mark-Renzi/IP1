Help Ninja-Se Escape!

Author:

Mark Renzi

GithubLinkedIn

Instructions:

You can use the arrow keys or the on-screen buttons to move the character. You can pick up keys using the space bar or the on screen button, and unlock doors by walking into the while holding the correct key. The goal is to unlock all of the doors to help Ninja-Se escape.
